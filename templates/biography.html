About the Artist

Allen Mayo was born in 1989, in Memphis, TN. There he built lifelong relationships with friends and family until his departure to Alaska in 2009. There he began working at a local sporting goods store specializing in hunting, fishing, camping and outerwear. As Allen works there, he continues to make new friends and acquire knowledge and stories from those around him. Good friends and wonderful artists Kathleen and Gabe Brown inspired Allen to start painting in 2014. Understanding he was not where he wanted to be with a brush, Allen switched to using a palette knife with oil paints. Allen's landscape paintings are inspired by the images provided by Anthony Madden Photography here in Anchorage.
